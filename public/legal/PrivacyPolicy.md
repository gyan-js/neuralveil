# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 30, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 30, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

NeuralVeil (neuralveil.dev) is an ML developer tools platform built and maintained by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the Service, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

NeuralVeil is designed with a privacy-first approach. The core tools run entirely client-side in your browser — your model architectures, tensor shapes, and GPU configurations never leave your machine.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

The following data is **never transmitted to or stored on NeuralVeil's servers:**

- Neural network architecture definitions or model code you input into any tool
- Tensor shape configurations or computation graph data
- GPU memory estimation inputs or results
- Any uploaded or pasted PyTorch/Keras model code
- Shareable URLs generated by tools (these encode state in the URL itself, client-side only)

---

## 3. Information We May Collect

### 3.1 Analytics

NeuralVeil may use privacy-respecting, anonymized analytics to understand how the platform is used. This may include:

- Pages visited and tools used
- Browser type and approximate geographic region (country/region level)
- Referral source (e.g. GitHub, Reddit, HuggingFace)
- Session duration and general usage patterns

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

### 3.2 Feedback and Contact

If you submit feedback, a bug report, or reach out via a contact form, we collect the information you voluntarily provide (e.g. your email address and message). This is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

### 3.3 Hosting and Infrastructure

NeuralVeil is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect standard server-side request logs (IP addresses, request timestamps, user agent strings) as part of normal infrastructure operation. This is governed by [Netlify's Privacy Policy](https://www.netlify.com/privacy/).

---

## 4. Cookies and Local Storage

NeuralVeil may use browser local storage to persist your tool settings or preferences across sessions (e.g. saved architecture configurations, toggle states). This data stays on your device and is never synced to any server.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

NeuralVeil may integrate with or link to third-party services. These have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their data practices:

- **Netlify** — hosting and CDN
- **GitHub** — source code and issue tracking
- **HuggingFace Spaces** — tool demos (if applicable)
- **Pyodide / WebAssembly runtime** — runs entirely in your browser; no data is sent externally

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

NeuralVeil is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Service, please contact us and we will delete it.

---

## 7. Data Retention

Since NeuralVeil does not collect personal data in the normal course of tool usage, there is no user data to retain or delete. Feedback and contact form submissions may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your inquiry.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

- The right to access data we hold about you
- The right to request deletion of your data
- The right to object to processing

Since we collect minimal personal data, most of these rights are automatically satisfied. For any requests, contact us via the information on neuralveil.dev.

## 9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the Service and any information handled through it. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
